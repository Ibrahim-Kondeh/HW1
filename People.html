Breea Clark

Director, JCPenney Leadership Center
Department: JCPenney Leadership Program

Phone:(405) 325-2501
E-mail:breea@ou.edu
Address: 307 W. Brooks
Norman, OK 73019-4004

Breea Clark is originally from Wichita, Kansas, where she graduated with her B.A. in Political Science from Wichita State University in 2005. Before receiving her J.D. from the University of Oklahoma College of Law in 2008, she started working for the University’s Office of the Provost on the main campus and was instrumental in the creation of the Office of Academic Integrity Programs. As the current Director of the JCPenney Leadership Center, she advises the JCPenney Leadership Program and teaches leadership courses and Passport to Success workshops. On campus, she has served on the University’s Legal Panel, Speakers Bureau and as an interim Pre-Law Club Advisor. She is also a proud graduate of the 2018-19 OU Leadership Council class. In the Norman community, she served on the Norman City Council from 2016-2019 representing Ward 6 in NE Norman and currently serves as its first Millennial Mayor. She also serves as a member of the Oklahoma Municipal League Board of Directors and the Association of Central Oklahoma Governments Board of Directors. She also completed the 2018-19 Oklahoma Municipal League’s Community Leadership Development class and founded the Norman Eastside Business Association..
